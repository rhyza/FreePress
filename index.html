WHO ARE WE

UConn students who Give a Fuck®. Content without restrictions, without need to tiptoe around the controversial. We are the Free Press.

Find us on facebook and uconntact.

GET INVOLVED

Want to get involved? Submit content, get other people to submit content, email us, and/or come to our weekly meetings!

Weekly meetings for Fall 2015 are TBD.

YOUR WORK HERE

Send us your thoughts, artwork, music/book reviews, essays, poetry, photography, or whatever else you would like to see published! Our magazine survives on content made by UConn students like you.

Submit to freepress@gmail.com!

PUNX CALENDAR

Sometimes we hold events! Sometimes other people hold events! We'll keep you updated on our calendar.
Trying to get into the CT music scene? That's right, it exists and we know how hard it can be to find shows. So we'll also list shows on our calendar. Actually, our calendar is mostly shows.
